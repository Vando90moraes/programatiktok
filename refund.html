How to request a refund

Send an email to hello@programatiktok.com with:

  • The product name (e.g., "TikTok Growth Planner" or "Beauty Creator Blueprint")

You do not need to explain why — but if you have feedback, we welcome it. It helps us improve.

We process refunds within 2 business days of receiving your request. Stripe typically returns the funds to your card within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank. The exact timing is controlled by Stripe and your card issuer, not by us.

What happens after a refund

Once a refund is issued, your access to the corresponding product is revoked. If you change your mind later, you can purchase the product again at the current price.
