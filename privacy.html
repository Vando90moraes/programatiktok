Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information programatiktok.com (the "Site") collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Payment information

When you purchase a product, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers, CVV codes, or full billing details on our servers. Stripe collects and processes that information under its own privacy policy.

Email address

If you contact us via hello@programatiktok.com or are passed an email by Stripe at checkout, we may receive your email address solely for the purpose of communicating about your purchase or support request.

Browser storage

We use your browser's localStorage to remember whether you have purchased a product, so the Site can grant access without requiring a login. This data stays on your device — we do not transmit it to our servers.

Hosting logs

The Site is hosted on Vercel, which automatically logs basic request data (IP address, user agent, requested URL) for security and performance. We do not run additional analytics or tracking pixels at this time.

2. How We Use Information

  • To process your purchase and grant access to the product
  • To respond to support inquiries you initiate
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical or fraud-related issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on a small set of third parties:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Vercel — site hosting and CDN
  • Google Search Console — search indexing data (no user-level tracking)

Each operates under its own privacy policy.

4. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. The browser storage referenced above is not transmitted to us and exists only on your device.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including under GDPR in the EU and CCPA in California), you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of that data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@programatiktok.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain transactional records (via Stripe) for the period required by tax and accounting law. Support emails are retained as long as needed to provide ongoing assistance, and then deleted on request.

7. Children's Privacy

The Site is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top will be revised. Material changes will be highlighted on this page.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or requests: hello@programatiktok.com
